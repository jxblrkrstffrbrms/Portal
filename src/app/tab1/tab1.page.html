About

LCSINHS

LCSINHS Mission The LCSINHS shall enhance maturity and mold students inclination in Science and Technology for them to be productive citizens and be future leaders who will direct and help the country towards new undertakings and goals. The concept, objectives, goals, and educational performance of the LCSINHS can contribute a lot in the quality and standard of education. LCSINHS Vision A school to turn out Filipino youth with desirable traits, who are scientifically-inclined and can compete with other graduates and can serve as efficient future leaders. LCSINHS Motto “Excellence is not our goal, it is our standard.”
